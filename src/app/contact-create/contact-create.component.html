VOLUNTEER

We have a myriad of programs for the residents in our nursing home. Whether you can only serve once a week or once a month, join us for a fulfilling and heartwarming time of serving the elderly.

REMINISCENCE WITH US

Everyday | Time: 07:30am – 10am

Socialise with our residents by listening to them tell their stories. Walk down memory lane together with them as they share the journey of their past.

MORNING YOGA WITH US

Every Morning | Time: 05:45am – 7am

If you are interested in trying yoga, Start a daily routine based holistic practice with Senior's morning yoga class.

COOK WITH US

Every Sat-Sunday | Time: 12:30pm – 4pm

Love making, baking or cooking?
Spend time with our residents and whip up a delicious meal together!

MUSIC WITH US

Every Sunday | Time: 05:00pm – 7pm

If you love music, sing or even play music, come be the DJ and/or sing along together with the elderly!
