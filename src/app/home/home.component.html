Panch Ash Sewarth Sameeti (PASS) is a non-profit organization set up on June 19, 2018, by a group of like-minded professionals & Social from different walks of life pledged to raise the standard of living of the needy living in the urban slums and rural areas. We are an operational NGO, we plan and carry out boots-on-the-ground projects to accomplish our objectives. This requires a great deal of careful planning, communication, and local involvement for each project.


After retirement and into our later years, it’s actually a really great time to set personal goals because we’re no longer bound by responsibilities like work and raising children as we once were.

But we also want to make the most of the time and energy we invest.

Panch Ash Sewarth Sameeti is helping aging adults to strategize, develop plans and expectations in the direction of their goals. Pass is working with other helping bodies for senior citizens in bringing of their basic needs i.e. food, shelter, medical, living.

OUR MISSION

To elevate the life chances of young people from the areas of social and economic deprivation – with opportunities to help them succeed at the various levels of education, employment, and the professions

To transform the educational achievement of the nation’s young people, schools and academies

To conceive and operate the skill development programmes logically and systematically to improve various target groups

To make a demonstrable impact on the social mobility and equality of the disadvantaged

OUR WORKING MODEL

To establish, promote, set-up, run, maintain, assist, finance support and/or aid in setting up and/or maintaining and/or running school and other educational institutions, orphanages, widow homes, lunatic asylums, poorhouses, or other establishments for relief and help to the poor, old and infirm people and/or destitute.
